How to play

The game

1
Host creates a room and shares the code — friends join by typing it on the home screen.
2
Everyone adds photos from a trip or somewhere interesting — the app reads each photo's GPS, or you drop a pin on the map if it's missing.
3
Select your photos, tap Add to pool, then I'm ready. The host starts the game once everyone is ready.
4
Guess where each photo was taken by dropping a pin on the map. Tap the map to expand it first if it's too small.
5
Score up to 5,000 pts per photo — the closer your pin, the higher the score. Check the leaderboard and reveal map when you're done.

Guessing tips

Zoom & pan the photo to spot signs, landmarks, or clues — pinch on mobile, scroll wheel on desktop, double-tap to zoom in/out.
Tap the mini-map to expand it before dropping your pin. Tap × to shrink it back.
On desktop, press Enter or Space to confirm a guess or move to the next photo.
You have a time limit per photo (set by the host). If time runs out without a pin, you score 0 for that photo.
📍 Photo location
Safari strips a photo's GPS before the app can read it, so you'll set the location by hand — when no GPS is found, tap "Set location" and drop a pin on the map. For automatic detection, add your photos from Chrome.
